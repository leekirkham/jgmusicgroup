Sweet Cherry Pie
(80's Rock/Hair/Glam Tribute Band)

Take a musical ride to the 1980s when big hair, tight pants and excess in all things ushered in some of the most anthemic party rock of all time. Sweet Cherry Pie entertains audiences of all ages with the trademark party rock classics that defined an era that included bands like Bon Jovi, Poison, Mötley Crüe, Twisted Sister, RATT, AC/DC, Van Halen, Def Leppard and more.
