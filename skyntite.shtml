SkynTite
(70's Disco Show Band)

SkynTite is a 70's boogie nites themed disco show band performing the music from the 70's disco era. The band performs 1 ½ to 3 hour shows depending on venues requirements. This includes lighting, sound system, stage development and disco attire.
