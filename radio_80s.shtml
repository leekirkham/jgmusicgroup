Radio80s
(1980's Radio Hits Band)

Radio80s covers all the great, fun, dance and party hits that were popular in the 1980s. Radio80s plays rock, pop, disco, funk, reggae, performing at casinos, resorts, weddings, clubs, bars and more. The band is very interactive in there performances which includes the dress look and vibe of the 80s era .
